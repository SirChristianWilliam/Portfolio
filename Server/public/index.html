Who am I?

Welcome to my page! I'm twenty-seven years old and I have been professionally coding/programming for over two years now. I began my journey as a Software Developer back when I was ten years old, creating fun beginner projects on Scratch, a website that teaches young children the basics of code. I love learning and broadening my mind and skillset, which is why I transitioned out of the service industry to pursue something that would challenge me every day. Outside of my passion for problem solving, I also have a passion for people. I love sports, making new friends, and learning about different cultures. I'm a healthy mix of not too extroverted and not too introverted, which I feel aids in empathy being a strong trait of mine. I enjoy reading fantasy novels, rock climbing, comedy shows, watching movies that are so bad that they're good, and pretty much anything that gets me outside. You'll notice I've included examples of past projects I've made that I'm proud to showcase to you. Please feel free to look around!

Currently

Since August 15th, I have been attending an accelerated learning program called Prime Digital Academy. I have learned an absolutely insane amount of knowledge in a relatively short amount of time. Some of what I've learned includes: Javascript, React, Redux, C#, Postico, Postman, Jquery, PostgreSQL, Figma, Git/Github, VScode, AWS, Heroku, MUI, Node, npm, entity relationships, server/client side, Slack and Zoom communications, peer review, debugging, problem solving, and more. I've been putting in 60+ hours per week since August and have been loving every minute of it. Time management is definitely a skill I've honed well!
